# LinkedIn Profile Import - Mobile

Resume Master supports importing your basic profile information from LinkedIn to pre-fill your resume on iOS and Android.

## What Gets Imported
- Your full name
- Your email address
- Your profile photo, if LinkedIn provides one

## What Does NOT Get Imported
Work history, education, skills, and connections are not available through the LinkedIn import. Add those sections manually in the Resume Builder for the best results.

## How It Works
1. Tap "Import from LinkedIn" in the Resume Builder.
2. A secure browser opens LinkedIn's login page.
3. You approve the access request for name and email only.
4. You are returned to Resume Master with your fields pre-filled.
5. Your LinkedIn credentials and access token are never stored.

## Privacy
Resume Master does not store your LinkedIn credentials or access token. The import is a one-time read through LinkedIn's official OpenID Connect API. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
