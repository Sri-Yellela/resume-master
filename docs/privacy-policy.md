# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

## Data We Collect
Resume Master collects the information you provide directly: account credentials, profile information, resume content, generated resumes, job descriptions you submit for ATS scoring, and application tracking entries you create.

When you use LinkedIn import, we collect only the name and email you explicitly share via LinkedIn's OAuth consent screen. We do not access your connections, work history, skills, education, or private LinkedIn data.

## How We Use Your Data
Your data is used to provide the Resume Master service: resume building, template selection, PDF export, ATS analysis, manual application tracking, and profile pre-fill. We do not use your resume content or job data to train AI models.

Job listings are sourced from Adzuna and Indeed via their official publisher APIs. We do not scrape job boards.

## Chrome Extension
The Chrome extension reads only the visible job description text on supported job listing pages, and only when you click the Send to Resume Master button. It does not read your LinkedIn profile, does not auto-apply, and does not store user data.

## Applications
Applications are submitted manually by you via the official employer application page. Resume Master may track that you applied when you record the application, but it does not submit LinkedIn applications on your behalf.

## Third Party Services
Resume Master uses LinkedIn OpenID Connect for user-consented name and email import, Anthropic for AI resume generation and ATS analysis, Adzuna for job listings, and Indeed Publisher for job listings. Each service is used only for the feature you initiate.

## Data Storage and Security
Data is stored on the Resume Master server. Passwords are hashed before storage. Session tokens are stored server-side and expire on logout or inactivity. LinkedIn access tokens are discarded after the import request and are not written to the database or logs.

## Your Rights
You may request access to your data, delete your account, export your resume history, and opt out of non-essential data collection.

## Contact
For privacy-related inquiries, use the contact form at /contact. We will respond within 5 business days.
