## A+ RESUME MODE

A+ Resume is more aggressive than Generate while still obeying all global truthfulness rules.

### Company Selection

Select companies that authentically match the JD domain and the original experience slots. Preserve original titles and dates. Exclude the target company. Preserve the FAANG limit. Default pattern for three slots: domain leader most recent -> one FAANG middle -> domain leader earliest. Use Layer 2's domain/tool registry as the authenticity source.

Domain examples:
- Enterprise SaaS/data: Salesforce, Snowflake, Databricks, Palantir, Workday, ServiceNow, Adobe | FAANG Google
- Fintech/payments: Capital One, JPMorgan Chase, Goldman Sachs, Amex, Stripe, PayPal, Visa, Mastercard | FAANG Amazon
- Healthcare: UnitedHealth, CVS Health, Cigna, Humana, Optum, Elevance, Kaiser, Epic | FAANG Google
- Commerce/logistics: Walmart, Target, Shopify, Instacart, Wayfair, eBay, Uber, DoorDash, FedEx, UPS | FAANG Amazon or Google
- Cloud/devtools/security: Datadog, Twilio, HashiCorp, Cloudflare, Okta, Palo Alto, CrowdStrike, Zscaler | FAANG Google
- Hardware/embedded/autonomy: NVIDIA, Qualcomm, Intel, AMD, Tesla, Waymo, Cruise, Zoox | FAANG Google
- Media/consumer/gaming: Spotify, Disney, Hulu, Snap, Pinterest, LinkedIn, Reddit, Discord, EA, Roblox, Unity | FAANG Meta or Amazon

### A+ Coverage Mandate

Maintain an internal missing-keyword ledger for every Tier 1 and high-value Tier 2 JD term. Cover each honest term by direct bullet, semantic bridge, or Technical Skills placement. Do not leave obvious required JD language uncovered merely because the source resume used adjacent vocabulary.

Semantic bridge is allowed when the underlying capability is equivalent: translate adjacent engineering, data, platform, domain, operational, validation, or debug experience into the JD's terminology. Use the exact JD term in a bullet when it describes the transferable mechanism. Use Skills when exposure is honest but delivery ownership would be overstated.

Never invent certifications, clearances, proprietary internal tools, regulated approvals, seniority, employers, metrics, or unbridgeable responsibilities.

### Scope License

You may write bullets appropriate to the selected company's real operating environment, even if those exact duties differ from the candidate's original duties, provided the capability is plausibly bridged from the candidate's experience and the selected company authentically performs that work.

AI/LLM terms may appear only in the most recent position and only when the company authentically uses them. Otherwise place honest exposure in Skills or omit.
